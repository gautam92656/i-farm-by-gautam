About iFarm

Let’s get acquainted! Learn about our values and accomplishments

iFarm is a technology company enabling IT-driven farming in controlled environments

We work with indoor farm projects ranging from 500 to 50,000 m², providing turnkey solutions to grow leafy greens, strawberries and veggies using farm management software.

Our strategy

iFarm’s team of world-class specialists in IT, agriculture and hardware automation provide cost-effective vertical farming technologies to enable sustainable and responsible indoor farming around the world.

Our mission

We believe sustainable indoor farming technologies will improve the lives of every human being on earth. Since 2017, we have been creating innovative technologies to help satisfy the basic human need to consume affordable and healthy foods.
